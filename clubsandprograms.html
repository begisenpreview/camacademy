Clubs and Programs at CAM Academy

Enriching Student Life Through Sports and Events

At CAM Academy, we believe in providing a well-rounded education that extends beyond the classroom. Our sports programs and special events create opportunities for students to develop physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills while having fun.

Our Sports Programs

We offer a variety of sports programs designed to develop physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship:

Football

Our football program focuses on developing fundamental skills, tactical understanding, and team play. Students participate in regular training sessions and inter-school competitions.

Athletics

Our athletics program covers track and field events, helping students develop speed, endurance, and coordination while competing in various meets throughout the year.

Volleyball

The volleyball program emphasizes teamwork, coordination, and strategic thinking. Students learn both indoor and beach volleyball techniques.

Special Events

Throughout the year, we organize various events to engage students and create memorable experiences:

Bible Trivia

Our Bible Trivia events combine fun and learning, testing students' knowledge of biblical stories and teachings while fostering spiritual growth and community engagement.

Sports Days

Annual Sports Days bring together the entire school community for a celebration of athletic achievement, featuring various competitions, team events, and fun activities for all ages.

Annual Talent Show

A showcase of student talents with dance, drama, and music performances. Students get the opportunity to display their artistic abilities and creativity in front of the school community.

Debates and Elocution

Regular competitions that help students develop public speaking skills, critical thinking, and confidence. Students participate in both individual and team debates on various topics.
