Life at CAM Academy

A Vibrant Learning Community

At CAM Academy, we believe that education extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant community fosters growth, creativity, and character development in every student.

Our Holistic Approach

CAM Academy provides a rich environment where students can develop academically, socially, and personally. From our earliest learners to our graduating seniors, we create opportunities for every child to discover their unique potential.

Extracurricular Excellence

Our diverse range of activities includes:

Sports

Football, basketball, swimming, athletics, and more activities that promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Arts

Music, drama, visual arts, and creative expression opportunities for students to showcase their talents.

Clubs

Science club, debate team, environmental club, and leadership opportunities to develop diverse interests.

Community Service

Regular outreach programs and social responsibility initiatives to develop compassionate global citizens.

Our Core Values

At CAM Academy, we are guided by five fundamental principles:

Empathy

Understanding and caring for others, fostering a compassionate community where everyone feels valued.

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals, developing teamwork skills essential for future success.

Perseverance

Maintaining effort despite challenges, building resilience and determination in our students.

Creativity

Thinking innovatively and expressing unique ideas, encouraging students to explore new possibilities.

Independence

Developing self-reliance and personal responsibility, preparing students for life beyond school.

Academic Excellence

Our comprehensive curriculum follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) framework, ensuring that students develop not only knowledge but also essential skills for the 21st century. Our dedicated teachers create engaging learning experiences that challenge and inspire.

Join Our Community

Life at CAM Academy is about creating memories, building friendships, and developing the skills and character needed for future success. We invite you to be part of our vibrant learning community where every student has the opportunity to thrive.
